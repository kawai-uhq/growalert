How It Works

Report a Scammer

Submitting a report is quick and straightforward. Our team reviews every case carefully before listing anyone.

STEP 01
🎯
Join the Server

Head to our Discord server and go to the reports channel. Make sure you've read the rules before submitting.

STEP 02
📋
Open a Ticket

Use GrowHelper to open a report ticket. Provide the scammer's GrowID, evidence screenshots, and a description of what happened.

STEP 03
🔍
Staff Review

Our staff carefully reviews all submitted evidence. We verify claims before taking any action to ensure fairness.

💡

Good to know: False reports are taken seriously. Always provide accurate evidence. Verified scammers are listed publicly to warn the community.
